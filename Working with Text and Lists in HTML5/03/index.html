Welcome to Bethany's Pie Shop

Our history so far

For many years, Bethany has been baking the most delicious pies at her home. In 2013, we opened our first store in Brussels (Belgium) in a cosy little street near the Grand Market. People froma ll over the world fell in love with the osft cakes, heavenly cheese cakes, spicy apple pies... The store became a success in no time.

In 2015, no less than 100 pies were sold every day and Bethany's Pie Shop moved into a larger building after making a 2000 € revenue. Afer receving many requests it became clear that our next step was making it possible to order pies from the comfort of your own and from anywhere in the world. You are now looking at the result of this; our very own webstore, making it possible to order Bethany's delicious pies whenver you feel like it!
